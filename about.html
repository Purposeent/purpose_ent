Purpose Ent/Purpose Publishing Inc. are the inspirations of multi instrumentalist, musician, songwriter, Producer Carl James. Carl has worked with some of the worlds most noted and famous artist in the music industry. His talent has had him cover the gamut of genres, from recording bass tracks on Mariah Carey Emotion album to touring with Steve Windwood on his Higher Love tour, rocking out with Living Colour to recording with Tupac Shakur, James Brown, Marta Sanchez and Mecano from Spain. The list of his works go on. Not one to rest on his laurels, Carl was and has been fed by the need to be a part of really Good music, whatever the genre. Whether it be Pop, Rock, Jazz or Reggae, he has always strived to make exceptional music with his productions, thus he is labeled a perfectionist. In 1998 Carl Formed Purpose Publishing for the Sole purpose of developing and establishing his writing skills as well as those of young artist that had the talent but were not fully developed as yet. In 2007 Carl formed Purpose Entertainment. His goal and that of the company is to find that outlet for the talent that is so deserving of the stage and for the listening public to be made aware and fulfilled by this music.
